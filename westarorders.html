WeStarPrints

Here at WeStarPrints we are very focused on turning your ideas into a reality. Most orders are reviewed before replying back about order confirmation.

The resin printer we use is the CREALITY HALOT ONE. The build size on the printer is 120*80*160 mm or 4.7*3.1*6.2 in

We will create and review designs until you are satisfied with the final product. Pricing is not fixed and is dependant on the final print weight. Larger builds can be built but will cost extra. Please feel free to send any orders down below and we will try to respond as soon as possible.
